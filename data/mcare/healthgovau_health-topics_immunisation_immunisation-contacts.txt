## Booking vaccinations

You can [book a vaccination appointment](/node/1211) a range of vaccination
providers, including your local GP or community health clinic.

## Immunisation history statements

Visit Services Australia's website to:

  * request an [immunisation history statement](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/individuals/services/medicare/australian-immunisation-register/what-immunisation-history-statement) for your child
  * find out about the [Australian Immunisation Register](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/individuals/services/medicare/australian-immunisation-register).

## Government payments

Visit Services Australia's website to find out about [immunisation
requirements for government
benefits](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/individuals/topics/what-are-
immunisation-requirements/35396), including Child Care payments and Family Tax
Benefit Part A Supplement.

## Travel vaccinations

Visit the [Smart Traveller](http://www.smartraveller.gov.au/) website.

## Help or support

If you need help or support, you can:

  * contact your state or territory health service (see below)
  * find the answers to [common questions about immunisation](/topics/immunisation/getting-vaccinated "Getting vaccinated")

If you are deaf or have a hearing or speech impairment, you can use the
National Relay Service to access any of the phone numbers on this page.

Visit the [National Relay Service](http://www.relayservice.gov.au/) online or
call [1300 555 727](tel:1300555727).
