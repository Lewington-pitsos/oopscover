# MBS Review – Government responses

This collection outlines the changes made to the Medicare Benefits Schedule
(MBS) in response to the MBS Review Taskforce's recommendations.

This collection outlines changes implemented by the government to the Medicare
Benefits Schedule (MBS) in response to
[recommendations](/resources/collections/mbs-review-final-taskforce-reports-
findings-and-recommendations "MBS Review – Final taskforce reports, findings
and recommendations") made by the [MBS Review](/our-work/mbs-review "Medicare
Benefits Schedule \(MBS\) Review ") Taskforce.

As at 1 November 2022, the Australian Government has:

  * agreed to 926 taskforce recommendations
  * implemented 753 recommendations, involving changes to over 3,000 MBS items.
