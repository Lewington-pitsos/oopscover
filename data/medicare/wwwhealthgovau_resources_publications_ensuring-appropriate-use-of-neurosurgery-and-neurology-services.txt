# Ensuring appropriate use of neurosurgery and neurology services

This fact sheet outlines recent changes to Medicare-funded neurosurgery and
neurology services. These changes support best practice treatment and improve
patient safety, and represent the Government’s response to recommendations
made by the MBS Review Taskforce.
