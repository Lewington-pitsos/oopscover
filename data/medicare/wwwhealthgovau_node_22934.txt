# CDBS – Substantiating dental services provided under the schedule

This guideline can help you to substantiate Child Dental Benefits Schedule
(CDBS) services that you have claimed or that have been claimed through your
provider number. You must also show that consent to dental services and costs
has been obtained and recorded.
