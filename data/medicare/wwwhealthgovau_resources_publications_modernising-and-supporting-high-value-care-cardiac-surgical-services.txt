# Modernising and supporting high value care cardiac surgical services

This fact sheet outlines recent changes to Medicare-funded cardiac surgery
services. These changes support best practice treatment and improve patient
safety, and represent the Government’s response to recommendations made by the
MBS Review Taskforce.
