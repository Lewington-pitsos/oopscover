# Quality Use of Pathology Program (QUPP) project grants 2021–23

This document provides feedback to applicants for the Quality Use of Pathology
Program Projects grant round (2021–23) GO5049 that was open on GrantConnect
from 3 September 2021 to 19 October 2021.
