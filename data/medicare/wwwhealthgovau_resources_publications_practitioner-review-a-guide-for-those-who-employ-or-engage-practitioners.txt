# Practitioner review – a guide for those who employ or engage practitioners

Under the Health Insurance Act 1973 (Health Insurance Act), a person
(including a practitioner) engages in inappropriate practice if they
knowingly, recklessly or negligently cause or permit a practitioner who they
employ or otherwise engage to engage in inappropriate practice.
