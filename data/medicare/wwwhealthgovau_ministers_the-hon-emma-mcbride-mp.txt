**The Hon Emma McBride MP**  
Assistant Minister for Mental Health and Suicide Prevention  
Assistant Minister Rural and Regional Health

Emma McBride is the Federal Member for Dobell. She has served in the
parliament since 2016.

Emma is the Assistant Minister for Mental Health and Suicide Prevention, and
Assistant Minister for Rural and Regional Health.

[Read Minister McBride's biography](/ministers/the-hon-emma-mcbride-
mp/biography "Minister McBride's biography")

18 May 2023

Media release

The Australian Government is providing $136.0 million over four years, and
$36.0 million ongoing, for torture and trauma services to help support
refugees with their psychological issues arising from their often traumatic
experiences before arriving in Australia.
