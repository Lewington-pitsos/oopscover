## Editorial team

The editorial team consists of the editor, deputy editor, and a designer. The
editorial team is supported by an Editorial Advisory Board (EAB). Details of
the EAB's then-current composition can be found within any article published
in CDI during this year. The EAB's present-day composition, and a description
of the role of the EAB, is detailed on CDI's front page.
