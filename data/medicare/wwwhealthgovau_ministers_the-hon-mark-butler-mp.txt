**The Hon Mark Butler MP**  
Minister for Health and Aged Care

## Biography

Mark Butler has been the Labor Member for Port Adelaide and Hindmarsh in the
Federal Parliament since 2007 and is the Minister for Health and Aged Care.

Mark served as Minister for Ageing and Australia’s first Minister for Mental
Health in the Gillard Government. He has also held the ministries of Housing,
Homelessness, Social Inclusion, Climate Change, Water and the Environment.

[Read Minister Butler's biography](/ministers/the-hon-mark-butler-mp/minister-
butlers-biography "Minister Butler's biography")
