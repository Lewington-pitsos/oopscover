# Health Technology Assessment (HTA) Chairs Committee

The HTA Chairs Committee provides advice on HTA investments under the
Preventive and Public Health Research initiative of the Medical Research
Future Fund (MRFF).

## Role

The committee advises the [Health and Medical Research Office](/our-
work/medical-research-future-fund/about-the-mrff/mrff-
governance#organisations-that-help-manage-the-mrff "MRFF governance") on the
strategic priorities for research investment under the HTA stream, which is
part of the [Preventive and Public Health Research initiative](/our-
work/preventive-and-public-health-research-initiative "Preventive and Public
Health Research initiative"). The initiative is funded by [MRFF](/our-
work/medical-research-future-fund "Medical Research Future Fund").

The role of the committee is to:

  * identify, discuss, prioritise, and refine grant opportunities under the HTA stream
  * support the monitoring of MRFF grants that are relevant to [HTAs](/topics/health-technologies-and-digital-health/health-technology-assessments "Health technology assessments")
  * undertake other work as needed.

## Members

The Australian Government appoints various [HTA committees](/topics/health-
technologies-and-digital-health/health-technology-assessments#who-conducts-an-
hta "Health technology assessments"). The chairs of these committees make up
the membership of the HTA Chairs Committee.

The chair of the HTA Chairs Committee is Adriana Platona. She is the First
Assistant Secretary of our Technology Assessment and Access Division.
