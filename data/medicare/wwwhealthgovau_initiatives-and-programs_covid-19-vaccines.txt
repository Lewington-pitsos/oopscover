# COVID-19 vaccines

COVID-19 vaccines are recommended for everyone 5 years and older. Find out
more about getting vaccinated.

Learn how to book your vaccination, what to do if you don't have a Medicare

###  [ COVID-19 boosters ](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/getting-your-

Read the latest information and advice about booster doses from the Australian
Technical Advisory Group on Immunisation (ATAGI).

###  [ Get the facts about vaccines ](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/is-it-true)

Find accurate, evidence-based answers to questions or misinformation about
COVID-19 vaccines.

###  [ COVID-19 vaccines and aged care ](/our-
work/covid-19-vaccines/information-for-aged-care-providers-workers-and-
residents-about-covid-19-vaccines)

Protecting people who receive and work in residential, in-home and community
aged care is a priority. Find resources for care recipients, workers and
providers.

## Browse vaccine rollout information for

###  [ Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people ](/our-
work/covid-19-vaccines/indigenous)

Getting vaccinated protects you, your family, elders and community from
getting really sick with COVID-19, or even dying. Everyone 5 years and over
can get vaccinated now.

###  [ People in aged care ](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/information-for-aged-
care-providers-workers-and-residents-about-covid-19-vaccines)

Information about vaccines, including third and fourth doses for people who
receive or work in residential, in-home and community aged care services.

###  [ People with disability ](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/disability-sector)

Information about vaccines for those with disability and those who provide
disability care, or who work in the disability sector, and Australia-wide
vaccination centres for people with disability.

###  [ Vaccine providers ](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/advice-for-providers)

Find information about joining the COVID-19 vaccine rollout program, vaccine
storage and handling requirements, clinical considerations and advice.
