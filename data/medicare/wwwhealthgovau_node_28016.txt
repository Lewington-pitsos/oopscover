## About the subscription

This newsletter contains updates from the [MBS Review](/our-work/mbs-review
"Medicare Benefits Schedule \(MBS\) Review ") including:

  * the Government's progress in responding to the [MBS Review Taskforce](/committees-and-groups/mbs-review-taskforce "Medicare Benefits Schedule \(MBS\) Review Taskforce") recommendations
  * our progress in establishing Implementation Liaison Groups (ILGs) and developing advice to Government to implement agreed changes to MBS items.
  * details and links to communication material for stakeholders in the lead up to changes to MBS items.

## Who it is for

This newsletter is for people with an interest in the MBS Review and changes
to MBS items.

## How to subscribe

[Subscribe to MBS Review newsletter](https://health.us3.list-
manage.com/subscribe?u=1f52d99769eadbb98a6751dbd&id=d207dd2540)

You will receive an email requesting confirmation. Once you confirm, you will
be on the list.

## How to unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe or change your subscription preferences at any time, using
links at the bottom of each email.

## Privacy

See the [privacy notice](/using-our-websites/website-privacy-policy/privacy-
notice-for-the-mbs-review-newsletter "Privacy notice for the MBS Review
newsletter") for this service.
