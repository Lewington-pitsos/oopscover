# Data matching foundational – Privacy Impact Assessment – Executive summary

This Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) executive summary serves as a baseline
assessment of routine and ongoing data matching programs relating to Medicare
compliance by health providers. It is intended to cover a range of compliance
activities undertaken by the Department.
