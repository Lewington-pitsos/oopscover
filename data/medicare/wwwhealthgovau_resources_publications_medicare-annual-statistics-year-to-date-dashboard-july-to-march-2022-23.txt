# Medicare annual statistics – year-to-date dashboard (July to March 2022-23)

This infographic shows statistics about Medicare use, including the number of
fully paid and bulk-billed services used, the amount of benefits paid and the
types of health services people used from July to March 2022-23. Includes bulk
billing by Primary Health Network.
