# Medicare access for allied health professionals

For allied health professionals to access Medicare rebates for their patients
they must satisfy Medicare’s requirements.

## **Billing under Medicare** ****

To bill under [Medicare](https://www.health.gov.au/health-topics/medicare),
allied health professionals must:

  * Meet the qualification and registration requirements as set out in the national [Health Insurance (Allied Health Services) Determination 2014](https://www.legislation.gov.au/Details/F2022C01072)
  * be recognised by Services Australia as an [allied health professional in their field](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/recognition-allied-health-professional-to-provide-medicare-services?context=34076)
  * be in private practice and working in a private capacity
  * have a [Medicare provider number](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/organisations/health-professionals/services/medicare/medicare-benefits-health-professionals/apply-provider-number-or-prescriber-number/if-youre-health-professional) to bill for services listed on the [Medicare Benefits Schedule](http://mbsonline.gov.au) (MBS).

Services Australia has Medicare information for [allied health
professionals](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/allied-health-
professional-eligibility-requirements-to-access-medicare-
benefits?context=34076), including eligibility requirements and guides on bulk
billing. Its [Health Professional Education Resources
Gateway](http://medicareaust.com/MISC/MISCP02/index.html) also has guides on
MBS items for allied health professionals.

## **Prescribing under the PBS**

[PBS Online](https://www.pbs.gov.au/info/healthpro/explanatory-
notes/section1/Section_1_2_Explanatory_Notes) has information for
Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS) prescribers, including allied health
professionals.

## **Providing services under the MBS**

[MBS Online](http://www.mbsonline.gov.au/) lists the Medicare services
subsidised by the Australian Government and available to allied health
professionals.
