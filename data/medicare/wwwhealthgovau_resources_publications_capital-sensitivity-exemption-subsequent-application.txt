Publication date:

20 September 2022

Publication type:

Form

Audience:

Health sector

Language:

English

Description:

An application form for Diagnostic Imaging practices to apply for the
continuation of the payment of Medicare benefits for services provided on
diagnostic imaging equipment that has passed its effective life age due to
unforeseen circumstances in the ability to replace or upgrade the equipment.
This form is for use when applying for an extension to an existing approved
exemption.
