# National Arbovirus and Malaria Advisory Committee (NAMAC) annual reports

This page contains links to the NAMAC annual reports, which are published in
_Communicable Diseases Intelligence_. The reports also inlcude data on the
Sentinel Chicken Surveillance Programme, which is used to provide an early
warning of increased flavivirus activity in Australia. These annual reports
are available in PDF format and, for accessibility, in either HTML or
Microsoft Word format.

Sentinel Chicken Surveillance Programme annual reports have been published in
_Communicable Diseases Intelligence_ since 2001. Previously reports were
published bi-monthly. Reports are for financial years from July to June. These
reports are now included in the more comprehensive NAMAC reports above.
