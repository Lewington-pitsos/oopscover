# Medicare access for optometrists

For optometrists to access Medicare rebates for their patients they must
satisfy Medicare’s requirements.

## **Billing under Medicare**

To bill under [Medicare](https://www.health.gov.au/health-topics/medicare),
optometrists must:

  * complete [approved education](https://www.ahpra.gov.au/Accreditation/Approved-Programs-of-Study.aspx?ref=Optometrist)
  * be registered with the [Optometry Board of Australia](https://www.optometryboard.gov.au/)
  * be [recognised by Services Australia](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/recognition-optometrist-to-provide-medicare-services?context=34076) as an optometrist
  * be in private practice and working in a private capacity
  * have a [Medicare provider number](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/organisations/health-professionals/services/medicare/medicare-benefits-health-professionals/apply-provider-number-or-prescriber-number/if-youre-health-professional) to bill for services listed on the [Medicare Benefits Schedule](http://mbsonline.gov.au) (MBS)
  * Services Australia has Medicare information for optometrists, including eligibility requirements and guides on bulk billing. Its [Health Professional Education Resources Gateway](http://medicareaust.com/MISC/MISCP02/index.html) also has guides on MBS items for optometrists.

## **Prescribing under the PBS**

To prescribe medicines listed on the Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS),
optometrists must have a PBS prescriber number.

[PBS Online](https://www.pbs.gov.au/info/healthpro/explanatory-
notes/section1/Section_1_2_Explanatory_Notes) has information for PBS
prescribers, including optometrists.

## **Providing services under the MBS**

[MBS Online](http://www.mbsonline.gov.au/) lists Medicare services subsidised
by the Australian Government and available to optometrists.
