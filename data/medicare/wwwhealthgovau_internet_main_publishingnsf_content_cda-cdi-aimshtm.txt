# Aims of the Communicable Diseases Surveillance website and _Communicable
Diseases Intelligence_

This page highlights the aims of both the Communicable Diseases Surveillance
website and _Communicable Diseases Intelligence._

 __Page last updated: 18 June 2008

To disseminate information on the epidemiology and control of communicable
disease in Australia.  

## Objectives of _Communicable Diseases Intelligence_ ( _CDI_ )

      * To report on surveillance of communicable diseases of relevance to Australia 
      * To publish other articles relevant to communicable disease epidemiology in Australia. 
      * To provide information relevant to the surveillance, prevention and control of communicable disease in Australia.

  
  
  

## Objectives of the Communicable Diseases Surveillance website:

  * To provide a timely mechanism for the dissemination of summary data from the National Notifiable Diseases Surveillance System. 
  * To provide electronic copies of _CDI_ and of current communicable disease control documents. 
  * To provide access to epidemiologists in the Surveillance Section for general inquiries about communicable disease control in Australia. 
  * To provide electronic links to other useful communicable disease Websites in Australia and overseas.
