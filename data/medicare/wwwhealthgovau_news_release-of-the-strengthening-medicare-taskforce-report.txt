# Release of the Strengthening Medicare Taskforce Report

The Strengthening Medicare Taskforce Report is now available. It outlines a
vision for Australia’s primary care system of the future.

The Australian Government released the report of the [Strengthening Medicare
Taskforce](/committees-and-groups/strengthening-medicare-taskforce
"Strengthening Medicare Taskforce") on 3 February 2023.

The Australian Government has committed $750 million to deliver the highest
priority investments in primary care, in line with the recommendations of the
Strengthening Medicare Taskforce.

Access the [Strengthening Medicare Taskforce
Report](/resources/publications/strengthening-medicare-taskforce-report
"Strengthening Medicare Taskforce Report").

Read the [media release](/ministers/the-hon-mark-butler-
mp/media/strengthening-medicare-for-the-future "Strengthening Medicare for the
future") for more details.
