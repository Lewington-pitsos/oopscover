# Quality Use of Pathology Committee

The Quality Use of Pathology Committee provides advice on the Quality Use of
Pathology Program (QUPP).

## Role

The Quality Use of Pathology Committee:

  * oversees the strategic direction of QUPP
  * provides expert advice to the Australian Government on pathology quality initiatives under the program
  * provides expert advice that may inform the department’s consideration of funds for pathology quality priorities
  * promotes awareness of the quality use of pathology.

## Members

The committee includes pathology professionals, scientists, medical
practitioners, tertiary education representatives, pathology consumer
representatives and representatives from the department.
