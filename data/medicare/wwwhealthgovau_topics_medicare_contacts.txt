![](/themes/custom/hga/images/contact-aus-gov.png)

### [Professional Services Review (PSR) ](/contacts/professional-services-
review-psr?language=en)

Department of Health and Aged Care Portfolio Agency

The Professional Services Review (PSR) Agency aims to protect the Australian
public from the risks and costs associated with inappropriate practice within
Medicare and the Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS).

![](/themes/custom/hga/images/contact-aus-gov.png)
