# Physical, mental and financial support

There are a range of physical, mental and financial support services available
to Australians during the COVID-19 pandemic. Find out where you can get
support.

## Telehealth services

If you have a Medicare card you can access telehealth services via phone or
video. Find out more about [accessing telehealth
services](/node/11614#telehealth).

## Financial support

You may be eligible for financial support from [Services
Australia](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/individuals/services/centrelink)
or your [local state and territory government](/node/28006#other-financial-
support).

## Mental and emotional health

If the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic are making you feel anxious, stressed
and worried you can access [a range of mental support services](/health-
alerts/covid-19/support/mental-health "Mental health support").

## Physical wellbeing

It's important to take care of your physical wellbeing during the COVID-19
pandemic. The most important action you can take to protect your physical
health is to [get vaccinated](/node/28225#benefits-of-vaccination).

Learn more about [prioritising your physical
health](/resources/publications/coronavirus-covid-19-prioritise-your-physical-
and-mental-health "Coronavirus \(COVID-19\) – Prioritise your physical and
mental health").

## Support for matters other than health

See [australia.gov.au](https://www.australia.gov.au/) for links to information
on:

  * safety advice
  * financial support
  * business and employer information
  * support for individuals, families and communities
  * education and child care information

## **Staying informed**

To stay up to date on COVID-19:

  * read [what you need to know](/health-alerts/covid-19/symptoms "COVID-19 disease and symptoms")
  * monitor the [COVID-19 current situation and case numbers](/health-alerts/covid-19/case-numbers-and-statistics "Coronavirus \(COVID-19\) case numbers and statistics")
  * view the latest [COVID-19 news and media](/coronavirus-covid-19-latest-news "Coronavirus \(COVID-19\) latest news")
  * know [how to protect yourself and others](/health-alerts/covid-19/protect-yourself-and-others "Protect yourself and others from COVID-19")
