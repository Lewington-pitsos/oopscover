## Cataracts - MBS items

The following MBS item numbers are included in this clinical category. _The
following is an indicative list of MBS items that are covered in the clinical
category. Other MBS items not listed here may also be covered. If you cannot
find the item you are looking for, please speak with your insurer._

42698

     LENS EXTRACTION, excluding surgery performed for the correction of refractive error except for anisometropia greater than 3 dioptres following the removal of cataract in the first eye (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Cataracts/42698)
42701

     INTRAOCULAR LENS, insertion of, excluding surgery performed for the correction of refractive errorexcept for anisometropia greater than 3 dioptres following the removal of cataract in the first eye (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Cataracts/42701)
42702

     LENS EXTRACTION AND INSERTION OF INTRAOCULAR LENS, excluding surgery performed for the correction of refractive error except for anisometropia greater than 3 dioptres following the removal of cataract in the first eye (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Cataracts/42702)
42703

     INTRAOCULAR LENS or IRIS PROSTHESIS insertion of, into the posterior chamber with fixation to the iris or sclera (Anaes.) (Assist.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Cataracts/42703)
42704

     INTRAOCULAR LENS, REMOVAL or REPOSITIONING of by open operation, not being a service associated with a service to which item 42701 applies (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Cataracts/42704)
42705

     LENS EXTRACTION AND INSERTION OF INTRAOCULAR LENS, excluding surgery performed for the correction of refractive errorexcept for anisometropia greater than 3 dioptres following the removal of cataract in the first eye, performed in association with insertion of a trans-trabecular drainage device or devices, in a patient diagnosed with open angle glaucoma who is not adequately responsive to topical anti-glaucoma medications or who is intolerant of anti-glaucoma medication. (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Cataracts/42705)
42707

     INTRAOCULAR LENS, REMOVAL of and REPLACEMENT with a different lens, excluding surgery performed for the correction of refractive error except for anisometropia greater than 3 dioptres following the removal of cataract in the first eye (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Cataracts/42707)
42710

     INTRAOCULAR LENS, removal of, and replacement with a lens inserted into the posterior chamber and fixated to the iris or sclera (Anaes.) (Assist.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Cataracts/42710)
42713

     IRIS SUTURING, McCannell technique or similar, for fixation of intraocular lens or repair of iris defect (Anaes.) (Assist.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Cataracts/42713)
42716

     CATARACT, JUVENILE, removal of, including subsequent needlings (Anaes.) (Assist.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Cataracts/42716)
