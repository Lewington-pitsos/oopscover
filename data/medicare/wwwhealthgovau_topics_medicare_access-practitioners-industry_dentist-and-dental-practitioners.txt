# Medicare access for dentists and dental practitioners

For dentists and dental practitioners to access Medicare rebates for their
patients they must satisfy Medicare’s requirements.

## **Billing under Medicare**

To bill under Medicare, dentists and dental practitioners must:

  * complete[ approved education](https://www.health.gov.au/health-topics/dentists/education-and-support) – set out in the Health Insurance (Dental Services) Determination 2007
  * meet the [eligibility requirements for a dentist or dental practitioner](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/dental-practitioner-eligibility-requirements-to-access-medicare-benefits?context=34076)
  * be registered with the [Dental Board of Australia](https://www.dentalboard.gov.au/About-the-Board.aspx) (DBA)
  * be [recognised as a dental practitioner by Services Australia](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/recognition-dental-practitioner-to-provide-medicare-services?context=34076)
  * be in a private practice and working in a private capacity
  * have a [Medicare provider number](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/organisations/health-professionals/services/medicare/medicare-benefits-health-professionals/apply-provider-number-or-prescriber-number/if-youre-health-professional) to bill for services listed on the [Medicare Benefits Schedule](http://mbsonline.gov.au) (MBS) and [Child Dental Benefits Schedule](https://www.health.gov.au/initiatives-and-programs/child-dental-benefits-schedule) (CDBS)
  * have a [PBS prescriber number](https://www.servicesaustralia.gov.au/how-to-apply-for-provider-number-if-youre-health-professional?context=34076) to prescribe medicines listed on the [Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme](https://pbs.gov.au) (PBS).

Eligible dentists and dental practitioners can:

  * provide services for certain items under the MBS
  * write prescriptions for certain medicines under the PBS.

Dental prosthetists can’t use their provider number for billing.

## **Prescribing under the PBS**

[PBS Online](https://www.pbs.gov.au/info/healthpro/explanatory-
notes/section1/Section_1_2_Explanatory_Notes) has information for PBS
prescribers, including dentists and dental practitioners.

## **Providing services under the MBS**

[MBS Online](http://www.mbsonline.gov.au/) lists Medicare services subsidised
by the Australian Government and available to dentists and dental
practitioners.
