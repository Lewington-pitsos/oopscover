## Dialysis for chronic kidney failure - MBS items

The following MBS item numbers are included in this clinical category. _The
following is an indicative list of MBS items that are covered in the clinical
category. Other MBS items not listed here may also be covered. If you cannot
find the item you are looking for, please speak with your insurer._

13100

     SUPERVISION IN HOSPITAL by a medical specialist ofhaemodialysis, haemofiltration, haemoperfusion or peritoneal dialysis, including all professional attendances, where the total attendance time on the patient by the supervising medical specialist exceeds 45 minutes in 1 day [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Dialysis/13100)
13103

     SUPERVISION IN HOSPITAL by a medical specialist ofhaemodialysis, haemofiltration, haemoperfusion or peritoneal dialysis, including all professional attendances, where the total attendance time on the patient by the supervising medical specialist does not exceed 45 minutes in 1 day [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Dialysis/13103)
13104

     Planning and management of home dialysis (either haemodialysis or peritoneal dialysis), by a consultant physician in the practice of his or her specialty of renal medicine, for a patient with end-stage renal disease, and supervision of that patient on self-administered dialysis, to a maximum of 12 claims per year [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Dialysis/13104)
13106

     DECLOTTING OF AN ARTERIOVENOUS SHUNT [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Dialysis/13106)
13109

     INDWELLING PERITONEAL CATHETER (Tenckhoff or similar) FOR DIALYSISINSERTION AND FIXATION OF (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Dialysis/13109)
13110

     INDWELLING PERITONEAL CATHETER (Tenckhoff or similar) FOR DIALYSIS , removal of (including catheter cuffs) (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/Dialysis/13110)
