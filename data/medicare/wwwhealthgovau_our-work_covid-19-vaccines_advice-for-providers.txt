# Advice for COVID-19 vaccine providers and administrators

Find information about vaccine storage and handling requirements, how to
manage your bookings, what you can and can't charge for, and resources to give
to patients.

  * [Becoming a COVID-19 vaccine provider](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/advice-for-providers/becoming-a-provider "Becoming a COVID-19 vaccine provider")  
Find out who can administer vaccines and where to access training.

  * [Vaccine allocations, storage and handling](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/advice-for-providers/allocations-storage-handling "COVID-19 vaccine allocations, storage and handling")  
Learn about your vaccine allocation, how to manage your allocation, and what
to do with expired or excess vaccines.

  * [Managing your bookings](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/advice-for-providers/managing-bookings "Managing your COVID-19 vaccination bookings")  
Find out how to manage bookings to vaccinate your new and existing patients.

  * [Clinical considerations](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/advice-for-providers/clinical-guidance "ATAGI clinical guidance for COVID-19 vaccine providers")  
Information to help vaccine providers determine eligibility and weigh up
clinical considerations for those seeking vaccination.

  * [Charging for consultations about vaccination](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/advice-for-providers/charging-medicare "Charging for consultations about COVID-19 vaccination")  
Vaccines are free for everyone in Australia regardless of Medicare or visa
status. Find out what services you can charge related to COVID-19 vaccination.

  * [Vaccinating your patients](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/advice-for-providers/vaccinating-patients "Vaccinating your patients against COVID-19")  
What to provide your patients, how to check the AIR, reporting vaccinations,
charging, DVA patients, reporting adverse events.

  * [Vaxzevria (AstraZeneca) vaccine and thrombosis with thrombocytopenia (TTS)](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/advice-for-providers/clinical-guidance/tts "Vaxzevria \(AstraZeneca\) vaccine and thrombosis with thrombocytopenia \(TTS\)")  
Clinical advice on the AstraZeneca vaccine and thrombosis with
thrombocytopenia (TTS).

  * [Comirnaty (Pfizer) and Spikevax (Moderna) and cardiac inflammation](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/advice-for-providers/clinical-guidance/myocarditis-pericarditis "COVID-19 vaccines and cardiac inflammation")  
Clinical advice on mRNA vaccines and the rare side effects of myocarditis or
pericarditis.

  * [Resources for COVID-19 vaccine providers](/our-work/covid-19-vaccines/advice-for-providers/resources "Resources for COVID-19 vaccine providers")  
Resources for vaccine providers to use when talking with patients about
COVID-19 vaccines. It includes links to resources for Aboriginal and Torres
Strait Islander patients, and translated resources for those who speak
community languages.
