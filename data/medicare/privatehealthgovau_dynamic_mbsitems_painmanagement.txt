## Pain management - MBS items

The following MBS item numbers are included in this clinical category. _The
following is an indicative list of MBS items that are covered in the clinical
category. Other MBS items not listed here may also be covered. If you cannot
find the item you are looking for, please speak with your insurer._

18280

     Sphenopalatine ganglion, injection of an anaesthetic agent, not in association with a service to which an item in Group T8 applies, unless the nerve block is performed using a targeted percutaneous approach (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/18280)
39013

     Injection of one or more zygo-apophyseal or costo-transverse joints with one or more of contrast media, local anaesthetic or corticosteroid under image guidance (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39013)
39014

     Medial branch block of one or more primary posterior rami, injection of an anaesthetic agent under image guidance (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39014)
39100

     Injection of primary branch of trigeminal nerve (ophthalmic, maxillary or mandibular branches) with alcohol, cortisone, phenol, or similar neurolytic substance, under image guidance (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39100)
39109

     Trigeminal gangliotomy by radiofrequency, balloon or glycerol, including stereotaxy (Anaes.) (Assist.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39109)
39110

     Left lumbar percutaneous zygapophyseal joint denervation by radio-frequency probe, or cryoprobe, using radiological imaging control Applicable to one or more services provided in a single attendance, for not more than 3 attendances in a 12 month period (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39110)
39111

     Right lumbar percutaneous zygapophyseal joint denervation by radio-frequency probe, or cryoprobe, using radiological imaging control Applicable to one or more services provided in a single attendance, for not more than 3 attendances in a 12 month period (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39111)
39116

     Left thoracic percutaneous zygapophyseal joint denervation by radio-frequency probe or cryoprobe using radiological imaging control Applicable to one or more services provided in a single attendance, for not more than 3 attendances in a 12 month period (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39116)
39117

     Right thoracic percutaneous zygapophyseal joint denervation by radio-frequency probe, or cryoprobe, using radiological imaging control Applicable to one or more services provided in a single attendance, for not more than 3 attendances in a 12 month period (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39117)
39118

     Left cervical percutaneous zygapophyseal joint denervation by radio-frequency probe, or cryoprobe, using radiological imaging control Applicable to one or more services provided in a single attendance, for not more than 3 attendances in a 12 month period (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39118)
39119

     Right cervical percutaneous zygapophyseal joint denervation by radio-frequency probe, or cryoprobe, using radiological imaging control Applicable to one or more services provided in a single attendance, for not more than 3 attendances in a 12 month period (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39119)
39121

     PERCUTANEOUS CORDOTOMY (Anaes.) (Assist.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39121)
39124

     CORDOTOMY OR MYELOTOMY, partial or total laminectomy for, or operation for dorsal root entry zone (Drez) lesion (Anaes.) (Assist.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39124)
39140

     EPIDURAL CATHETER, insertion of, under imaging control, with epidurogram and epidural therapeutic injection for lysis of adhesions (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39140)
39323

     Percutaneous denervation (excluding medial branch nerve) by cryotherapy or radiofrequency probe, other than a service to which another item applies, applicable not more than 6 times for a given nerve in a 12 month period (Anaes.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/39323)
45939

     PERIPHERAL BRANCHES OF THE TRIGEMINAL NERVE, cryosurgery of, for pain relief (Anaes.) (Assist.) [](/dynamic/MBSItems/PainManagement/45939)
