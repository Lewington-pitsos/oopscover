# Radiation Oncology Health Program Grants Scheme – Administrative guidelines

The Radiation Oncology Health Program Grants Scheme helps public and private
radiation oncology providers in areas of greatest need to buy eligible
radiation therapy equipment.

Description:

This document provides information about:

  * legislative arrangements
  * priority areas
  * application process
  * assessment criteria
  * procedural fairness for all applicants
  * funding
  * revocation of approvals.

See the application forms for:
