## MBS item numbers

Select one of the following clinical categories to see the MBS item numbers it
includes. See [Clinical
categories](/health_insurance/howitworks/clinical_categories.htm "Clinical
categories") for details of what they cover. Note: Common and support MBS
items are not included in these lists – see [MBS
Online](http://www.mbsonline.gov.au/ "Medicare Benefits Schedule Online") for
more information.

right of any page.
