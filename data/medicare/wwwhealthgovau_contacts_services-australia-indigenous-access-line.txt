# Services Australia Indigenous Access Line

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Australians can call this free hotline
if they need help with a Medicare service or payment (Monday to Friday 8:30am
to 5pm). Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander health workers can also use
this service. Staff working on the Access Line have cultural training.
