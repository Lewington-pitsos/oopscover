# Medical doctors and specialists

There are over to 130,000 medical doctors and specialists in Australia. Find
out about their work, what we’re doing to increase their numbers in rural and
remote areas, and how we support this critical part of our health workforce.

### National Medical Workforce Strategy

The National Medical Workforce Strategy guides long-term medical workforce
planning across Australia. This 10-year strategy will improve access to health
care by supporting the right people to have the right skills, where we need
them most.

[Read about the strategy](/node/27455)
