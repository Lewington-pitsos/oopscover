# Legislation we administer

The Health and Aged Care portfolio administers laws on behalf of our
ministers. These are set out in the Administrative Arrangements Order.

The [Administrative Arrangements Order](https://www.pmc.gov.au/resource-
centre/government/aao-29-may-2019) sets out the laws that ministers are
responsible for administering.
