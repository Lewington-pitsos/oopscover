# Medical and health students and trainees

Students can choose from a wide range of healthcare careers. Read about how
our policies, strategies and programs support medical and health students and
trainees through their study pathways, to help create a highly-skilled health
workforce for the future.

### For international medical graduates

DoctorConnect helps international medical graduates understand the steps
required to work as a doctor in Australia, where they can work and
restrictions on billing patients under Medicare.

[DoctorConnect](https://www.health.gov.au/initiatives-and-
programs/doctorconnect)
