# CDBS – Guide to the Child Dental Benefits Schedule

This guide is for dentists and explains the arrangements and requirements for
the Child Dental Benefits Schedule (CDBS).

Health sector

English

Description:

The guide covers:

  * the Child Dental Benefits Schedule
  * eligibility to provide Child Dental Benefits Schedule services
  * children eligible for dental services
  * how the patient’s benefit cap works
  * dental services covered by the schedule
  * quoting for services
  * charging, billing and claiming for dental services
  * using private health insurance for CDBS services.

This guide is not a legal document. In cases of discrepancy the legislation
will be the source document for the requirements of the program.
