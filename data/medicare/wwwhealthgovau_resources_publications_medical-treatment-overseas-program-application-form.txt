# Medical Treatment Overseas Program – Application form

Use this form to apply for financial support under the Medical Treatment
Overseas Program. The program covers the cost of approved treatment, airfares
and accommodation for approved Australians with a life-threatening condition
who need life-saving medical treatment that’s only available overseas.
